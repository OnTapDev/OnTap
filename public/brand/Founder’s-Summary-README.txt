OnTap – Founder’s Summary Sheet
🚀 Core Vision

OnTap is the all-in-one platform built for mobile bartenders — combining business tools + marketplace to help bartenders grow from freelancers into full-fledged mobile bar companies.
Think of it as the Uber/Toast/HoneyBook of mobile bartending.

🛠️ Product Phases

Phase 1 – Tools-First

Scheduling, payments, invoicing, portfolio builder

Branding + logistics support

Early bartenders become power users

Phase 2 – Marketplace

Activate job marketplace (private hosts, venues, event planners)

Priority placement for early users

Future-Proofing: Planned AI integrations (smart booking, pricing, automation).

💰 Monetization

Subscription Tiers

Founder - Before Full Launch ($20)

Free – Starter Profile

Pro – Solo Business Builder ($40)

Enterprise – Mobile Bar HQ ($150)

Booking Fee: 5–10% per gig (early adopters lock in 0%).

Projected ARPU: ~$75–80/month at full release.

📊 Market Position

Niche dominance: first-mover in mobile bartending SaaS + marketplace.

Adjacent tools exist (HoneyBook, Toast, gig apps) but none dedicated to mobile bartending.

Positioned as category leader in an underserved vertical.

📈 Funding & Growth

Pre-seed raise: $350K (legal, design, dev, marketing).

Growth strategy: Supply-first → Bartenders onboarded before marketplace opens.

Long-term exit: IPO, possible acquisition if price is right.

Plan B: Bootstrap, if traction is great enough to preserve equity.

🎨 Brand Identity

Aesthetic: Charcoal + Warm White and Variety Golds (luxury, modern, professional).

🥂 Mission Statement

OnTap empowers mobile bartenders to professionalize, grow, and thrive.
From solo freelancers to multi-bartender companies, OnTap is the definitive ecosystem for running and scaling a mobile bar business.
